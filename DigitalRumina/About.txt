Digital Rumina is an advanced security system utilizing IoT technology to enhance efficiency and reduce costs compared to traditional surveillance. It employs an Arduino UNO and IR sensors to detect motion, activating recording only when necessary. The system features Bluetooth control for remote management and includes LEDs and an LCD for real-time feedback. A relay allows integration with high-power devices like lights or alarms. Future upgrades may include facial recognition and cloud storage. Overall, Digital Rumina offers a versatile, scalable, and cost-effective solution for modern security needs.
